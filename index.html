Why This Exists

process, not lack of information.

Markets are full of data. What's missing is a structured way to think, decide, and act — without emotion or guesswork.

What AlphaLense Does

A structured way to think about markets.
Not a get-rich-quick tool.

AlphaLense is a rule-based, open-source strategy backtester. It tests how a disciplined investing approach would have performed on real NSE/BSE stocks — across your chosen time period.

📋
Rule-Based Entry & Exit
Defined buy triggers (5% step-down), exit rules (15% rise), and stop-loss (40% fall). No ambiguity, no emotion.
⚖️
Position Sizing Built In
Choose a fixed amount, 1% of capital, or dynamic sizing. Know exactly how much you're risking before you deploy capital.
📅
Historical Backtesting
Run your strategy on years of real NSE/BSE data. See CAGR, Sharpe ratio, max drawdown, and monthly P&L before risking real money.

Key Features

Everything you need to invest with discipline.

Six focused capabilities designed for retail investors who want structure, not speculation.

📋

Rule-Based Workflow

Every buy and sell follows a defined system. No discretion, no emotion — just consistent rules applied across thousands of historical candles.

📡

Live Market Data

Pulls real OHLCV data from Yahoo Finance for any NSE or BSE stock. Always up to date, no manual CSV uploads needed.

🧠

Better Decision Structure

Know your max lots, stop-loss levels, and position size before you enter any trade. Decision support that makes you a better investor.

🇮🇳

Retail-Friendly Interface

Built for ordinary Indians, not institutional traders. Clean, intuitive UI with sector-wise stock selection and preset Nifty 50 portfolios.

🔓

Open-Source Transparency

Every line of strategy logic is visible and auditable. No black box, no hidden fees. You can see exactly how every decision is made.

🤝

Community-Driven

Built in public, improved by feedback. Every suggestion, bug report, and idea from the community shapes what AlphaLense becomes next.
